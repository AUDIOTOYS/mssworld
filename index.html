Strategic Recommendations

Phase 1: Stabilize the Foundation

Timeline: Next 30-60 Days

This phase is critical for stopping operational bleeding and establishing control. The focus is entirely on the DO FIRST items to create a stable base for future growth.

  • Implement digital inventory and financial tracking immediately.
  • Standardize core safety, transport, and power SOPs.
  • Clarify team roles, improve communication channels (e.g., Slack), and establish accountability for gear handling.
  • Hire a technically competent warehouse manager.

Phase 2: Optimize & Systematize

Timeline: Next 2-6 Months

With basic controls in place, this phase focuses on efficiency, quality, and team development. Address all SCHEDULE and DELEGATE items.

  • Develop and begin structured training and upskilling programs.
  • Begin documenting business processes and tracking event profitability.
  • Create a company website and begin leveraging past work for marketing.
  • Invest in physical infrastructure like a maintenance room and better transport gear.

Phase 3: Strategic Growth

Timeline: 6-12 Months & Beyond

Once operations are smooth and data is being collected, focus on long-term strategy and market positioning. This phase builds on a solid foundation.

  • Use collected data (profitability, gear usage) to create a formal business model and investment roadmap.
  • Build a strong company brand identity separate from individuals.
  • Explore strategic gear investments based on trend data.
  • Re-evaluate and consider automation opportunities now that workflows are mature.
